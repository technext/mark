Project Details

Introduction

Evento is a mobile app that allows users to purchase e-tickets for local events using their phone. Users can save ticket and access app even being offline. App also provides reccomends events based on users interests.

Problem Statement

By conducting a survey prom local event organizers we found out that majority of event organizers face difficulties and challenges with ticket sales & event promotion related tasks. Usually they end up spending a lot of money on printing tickets and a lot of people are involved in entrance control carefuly checking tickets. Survey conducted by event attenders revelead that paper tickets usually unconvinient and they would love to be able to purchase the e-tickets from their phone.

Goals

  • 1

    Design and develop an app that will benefit both event organizers and event attenders making ticket selling/ booking process easier

  • 2

    Online ticket sales should decrease promotion & organization costs: the organizer does not spend resources on receiving and processing payments and less people can be involved in entrance checks since QR code based tickets will be scanned

  • 3

    Event attenders can register and save their payment methods which will enable them to purchase tickets with just couple clicks

Target Audience

People aged between 18-35 years old
All kinds of occupations
All genders and nationalities
